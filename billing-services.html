Medical Billing Services

Assisting Healthcare Providers

Medserv offers unprecedented transparency, dependability, and customer service to healthcare providers across all specialties, helping to increase payment turnarounds and reduce bad debt levels.

Speak to an expert Book a demo

Busy healthcare providers trust us to handle private patient billing. With years of experience, a professional team, and cutting-edge technology, we simplify your billing process and ensure you get paid for the work you do.

We make administration easier by:

  • Liaising directly with your accountant for straightforward returns
  • Integrating seamlessly with Xero (even for in-person practices)
  • Offering tax-efficient solutions—our fees are often tax-deductible, making them more affordable than they appear
  • Providing a simpler alternative to employing a secretary
  • Assisting with registration with insurers
  • Supporting your transition to a company structure, including insurer registration adjustments

Invoicing

Taking the stress out of paperwork is one of the many reasons our clients like our service so much. Submitting invoices for insurers and patients becomes our concern, along with:

  • Coding and pricing to insurance company guidelines
  • Liaising with patients and insurers regarding any invoicing queries
  • Follow-up and issue resolution
  • Patient online payment facilities

Reporting

We use our custom built online live reporting system to inform you of the progress of each and every invoice submitted. You can also:

  • Use the website to take a credit or debit card payment
  • Verify patient insurance details where possible
  • Create customised reports for complete visibility of your practice
  • Find the latest fee assured pricing from all major insurance companies

Account Administration

We will ensure that you are kept up to date on all progress and we will communicate our results to you in several ways: via our website on a 24/7 basis, on a monthly basis through our monthly reports and on an annual basis when tax return time comes around. In addition, our team are always on the other end of the phone to answer any and all questions you might have.

  • Comprehensive reporting
  • End of year accounting
  • Maintaining digital copies of all correspondence
