Simplify Group Billing.
Strengthen Your Practice.

Whether you’re forming a new group or managing an established one, Medserv makes private patient billing seamless. From setting up income distribution to navigating insurer pricing and real-time reporting, we tailor solutions to support every consultant and ensure your group runs smoothly, both financially and operationally.

Advice on group formation

When you are setting up a group, there are many questions and decisions to be made. We have assisted many groups from formation stage and can help you make the best choices for your group in order to make your billing process run smoothly.

Distribution of income

When you are operating as a group you want to ensure that all monies end up in the correct bank accounts. This can become complicated if you have to deal with both practice income and individual income. We have assisted groups in all of these areas and our custom systems are designed to deal with all possible scenarios.

Reporting to individual group members

Our reporting system is second to none. You can see in real time where you are in relation to your billing levels.

Registration with insurers

Within group structures, issues can arise with consultants charging different prices depending on the insurance company. We will set up the pricing structure for each individual consultant on our system. This reduces issues with various insurers and ensures swift payment for procedures that are carried out.
