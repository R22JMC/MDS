Founded in 2005

Medserv is an Irish company that was established by Cormac Loughrey in 2005 and has rapidly grown to become one of the largest companies specialising in medical billing in Europe.

Our Clients

Our clients include hospital consultants of all specialties, GPs, clinics as well as hospitals. We have clients in all major hospitals throughout Ireland and the UK and links with all main health insurers as well as the HSE and NHS.

GDPR

Given the sensitive nature of the information we process we have to be sure that we have all the necessary processes and checks in place when it comes to Data Protection and GDPR compliance.

We have been audited by the Data Protection Commission in Ireland and are voluntarily audited to test the robustness of our IT security systems. Protecting the sensitive information we receive is a primary focus for us at all times.

Systems
